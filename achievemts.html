Awards and Achievements of Sachin Tendulkar

Bharat Ratna

India's highest civilian award, awarded in 2014 for his contribution to Indian cricket.

Padma Vibhushan

The second-highest civilian award in India, awarded in 2008 for his contribution to Indian cricket.

ICC Hall of Fame

Inducted into the International Cricket Council's Hall of Fame in 2019 for his outstanding contribution to cricket.

Wisden Cricketer of the Year

Named Wisden Cricketer of the Year in 1997, becoming the first Indian to receive the award.
